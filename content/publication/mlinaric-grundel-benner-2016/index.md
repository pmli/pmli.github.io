abstract: In this paper, we extend our clustering-based model order reduction method
  for multi-agent systems with single-integrator agents to the case where the agents
  have identical general linear time-invariant dynamics. The method consists of the
  Iterative Rational Krylov Algorithm, for finding a good reduced order model, and
  the QR decomposition-based clustering algorithm, to achieve structure preservation
  by clustering agents. Compared to the case of single-integrator agents, we modified
  the QR decomposition with column pivoting inside the clustering algorithm to take
  into account the block-column structure. We illustrate the method on small and large-scale
  examples.
