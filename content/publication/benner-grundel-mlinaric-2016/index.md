abstract: |
  We develop a stability preserving model reduction method for linearly coupled
  linear time-invariant (LTI) systems. The method extends the work of
  Monshizadeh et al. for multi-agent systems with identical LTI agents. They
  propose using Bounded Real Balanced Truncation to preserve a sufficient
  condition for stability of the coupled system. Here, we extend this idea to
  arbitrary linearly coupled LTI systems using the sufficient condition for
  stability introduced by Reis and Stykel. The model reduction error bounds for
  this method also follow from results of Reis and Stykel, which allows the
  adaptive choice of reduced orders. We demonstrate the method on Reis's and
  Stykel's coupled string-beam example.
