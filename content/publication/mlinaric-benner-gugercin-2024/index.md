abstract: >-
  Interpolatory necessary optimality conditions for $\mathcal{H}_2$-optimal
  reduced-order modeling of non-parametric linear time-invariant (LTI) systems
  are known and well-investigated. In this work, using the general framework of
  $\mathcal{L}_2$-optimal reduced-order modeling of parametric stationary
  problems, we derive interpolatory ${\mathcal{H}_2 \otimes
  \mathcal{L}_2}$-optimality conditions for parametric LTI systems with a general
  pole-residue form. We then specialize this result to recover known conditions
  for systems with parameter-independent poles and develop new conditions for a
  certain class of systems with parameter-dependent poles.
