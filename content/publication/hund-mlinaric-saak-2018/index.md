abstract: >-
  So far, $\mathcal{H}_2 \otimes \mathcal{L}_2$-optimal model order reduction
  (MOR) of linear time-invariant systems, preserving the affine parameter
  dependence, was only considered for special cases by Baur et al in 2011. In
  this contribution, we present necessary conditions for an $\mathcal{H}_2
  \otimes \mathcal{L}_2$-optimal parametric reduced order model, for general
  affine parametric systems resembling the special case investigated by Baur et
  al.
